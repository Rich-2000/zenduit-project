Core Capabilities

Comprehensive Fleet Management Features

ZenUiT's platform provides everything you need to optimize your fleet operations, from real-time tracking to advanced analytics.

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Monitor your fleet's location in real-time with precision GPS tracking. Optimize routes, reduce fuel consumption, and improve dispatch efficiency with live location updates.

AI Dash Cameras

Advanced AI-powered dash cameras with 360° coverage detect unsafe driving behaviors, provide instant coaching, and capture critical incidents for enhanced fleet safety.

Predictive Maintenance

Prevent costly breakdowns with AI-driven predictive maintenance. Real-time diagnostics detect issues early, reducing downtime by up to 40% and extending vehicle lifespan.

Driver Behavior Analytics

Track and analyze driver performance with detailed metrics on speed, braking, acceleration, and idling. Implement targeted coaching to improve safety and reduce accidents.

Asset Security & Theft Prevention

Protect your valuable assets with geofencing, motion alerts, and remote vehicle shutdown capabilities. Recover stolen equipment quickly with hidden GPS trackers.

Compliance & Reporting

Automated compliance tracking and comprehensive reporting ensure adherence to safety regulations while providing actionable insights for continuous improvement.
